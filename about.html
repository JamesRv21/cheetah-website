About Us

Meet the dedicated team behind Scrum Zoo. We’re passionate about cheetahs and committed to conservation, education, and creating an unforgettable visitor experience.

Linus

Position: Head Cheetah Caretaker

Linus has over 10 years of wildlife management experience. He oversees daily feeding, health checks, and enrichment activities for our resident cheetahs. When he's not at the zoo, he publishes articles on big cat conservation.

Katelyn

Position: Education & Outreach Coordinator

Katelyn develops all educational programming, from guided tours to school workshops. She holds a degree in Zoology and loves creating interactive exhibits that teach visitors about cheetah ecology and conservation.

James

Position: Digital Media Manager

James handles Scrum Zoo’s website, social media, and multimedia campaigns. With a background in computer science and film, he produces our photo/video content and ensures our message reaches a global audience.
