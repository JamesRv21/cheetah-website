Mating and Reproduction Information

Cheetahs typically mate throughout the year, with no fixed breeding season. Males do not participate in raising young, and females raise cubs on their own.

  • Gestational Period: About 90–95 days.
  • Typical Litter Size: 3 to 5 cubs (can range from 1 to 8).
  • Parental Care Duration: Cubs stay with their mother for approximately 18 months, learning hunting and survival skills.
  • Interesting Fact: Cubs are born with a silvery mane along their back to help camouflage them in the grass—mimicking the honey badger!
